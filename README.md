# 🚨 NexusSOS – One Tap Emergency Response Platform

## Overview

NexusSOS is a GPS-powered emergency response platform designed to help users quickly locate and access critical emergency services during accidents and crisis situations.

During emergencies, valuable time is often lost searching for hospitals, ambulances, police stations, and rescue services across multiple applications. NexusSOS consolidates these services into a single platform, enabling users to discover, contact, navigate to, and share their location with the nearest emergency service providers.

---

## Problem Statement

During the Golden Hour after an accident or medical emergency, every minute matters.

People often face challenges such as:

* Searching for nearby hospitals
* Finding ambulance contact numbers
* Locating police stations
* Sharing their location with family or responders
* Navigating to the nearest emergency facility

These delays can impact emergency response effectiveness.

## Core Algorithm

### Haversine Formula

NexusSOS uses the Haversine Formula to calculate the shortest distance between two geographic coordinates.

This enables accurate ranking of nearby emergency services based on user location.
